Frequently asked questions

1.How can I book a ride with VOGY Cabs?
You can easily book a cab through our website or mobile app. Just select your pickup and drop locations, choose your ride type, and confirm your booking in seconds.
2.Are your rides available 24/7?
Yes, VOGY Cabs operates round the clock. Whether it’s an early morning airport drop or a late-night pickup, we’re always available to get you there safely.
3.What payment options do you accept?
We accept all major payment methods — cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets for your convenience.
4.Will I get driver and vehicle details before the ride?
Absolutely. Once your booking is confirmed, you’ll receive the driver’s name, contact number, and vehicle details for complete transparency and safety.
5.Can I modify or cancel my booking?
Yes, you can easily modify or cancel your booking through the website or app before the ride begins. Any applicable charges will be shown clearly before confirmation.
